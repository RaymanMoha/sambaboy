How Shambaboy Works

Shambaboy is both an app and a system ‚Äî designed to help farms function smoothly from the ground up. It simplifies daily operations so that everyone ‚Äî from owners to herders ‚Äî knows what to do, when, and how.

Supervisor Sees

"Collect livestock count from all pens (Morning)"

  • Logs headcounts
  • Uploads photo of signed logbook
  • Task is geo-tagged and timestamped

Herder Sees

"Confirm handover ‚Äì Pen A"

  • Counts cattle
  • Takes photo of signed logbook
  • Marks complete

Security Guard Sees

"Verify livestock count at handover ‚Äì Pen A"

  • Confirms tally
  • Signs off in the app

Owner Sees

Complete visibility and control

  • Herd count across pens
  • Photo evidence
  • Full log of who signed what, when, and where
  • Alerts for any missed tasks or mismatches

By 6:00 PM, a daily summary is generated automatically. No chasing people. No missing numbers. No stress.
